Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure product quality?

We personally inspect and verify every batch with growers, adhering to international standards and providing certificates upon request.

What is your minimum order quantity?

MOQ varies by product, typically starting from 20 metric tons, but we are flexible depending on the buyer’s needs.

Which countries do you export to?

We primarily serve buyers in Europe, the Middle East, and Asia, with established shipping routes and partners.

How does the brokerage process work?

We act as an intermediary: vetting sellers, matching them with buyers, negotiating terms, and overseeing secure fulfillment.
